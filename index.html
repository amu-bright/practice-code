highly processed foods

Highly processed food are processed or prepared food and drinks that add excess sodium, sugar, or saturated fat to the diet of canadians

  • How to limit highly processed food
    • replace sugary drinks with water
    • choose healthier menu option when eating out
    • make homemade versions of your favorite highly processed food
  • Highly processed foods include
    • sugary drinks
    • syrups and jams
    • chocolate and candies
    • potato chips and pretzels
    • sauces, dressings, gravies
    • Ice cream and frozen deserts
    • fast foods like French fries and burgers
